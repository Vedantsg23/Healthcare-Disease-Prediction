      Medicine Guide

      How to Use Medicines Safely

      Understand dosage, precautions, and when to seek medical help

      Always Read Labels

      Read the medicine packaging and leaflet fully before taking any medication. Check expiry dates and storage instructions.

      Follow Dosage Timing

      Take medicines at the right time — some must be taken with food, others on an empty stomach. Never skip or double doses.

      Drug Interactions

      Inform your doctor about all medicines you take. Some combinations can cause dangerous side effects or reduce effectiveness.

      Prescription Required

      Many medicines listed in this app require a doctor's prescription. Self-medicating for serious conditions can be dangerous.

      Stay Hydrated

      Drink plenty of water when taking most medicines unless advised otherwise. This helps your body absorb and process them correctly.

      Children & Elderly

      Dosage for children and elderly patients is often different. Always consult a paediatrician or geriatric specialist for these cases.

      About MediSense AI

      MediSense AI was developed as an intelligent healthcare companion that makes preliminary health information accessible to everyone — especially in areas with limited access to immediate medical consultation.

      The system uses a machine learning ensemble (Naive Bayes + Decision Tree + Random Forest) trained on clinical symptom-disease datasets, providing highly accurate predictions across 41 medical conditions.
